About Us

About The Vision

Our vision is to be a community of disciples making disciples at home and in the nations. We have three simple core values: Honor God. Love People. Make Disciples. Come join us to worship the Lord together in the heart of Nashville. You will experience new cultures, and learn more about how to follow Christ.

About The Movement

We are part of a global movement of churches called Every Nation. The vision of Every Nation is to establish Christ centered, spirit empowered and socially responsible churches and campus ministries in Every Nation. As part of this vision, we have been traveling the nations of the world preaching the Gospel and making disciples.

Meet The Pastors

Ps. Fikri & Margot Youssef

Every time we flew back home to Nashville we felt the need to plant an international church in town to reach Latinos and people from other nations living within our city. In January 2019 after the global fast where we joined churches from 80 nations to fast and pray, we felt compelled by the Lord to plant La Casa Church. The vision was to reach Latinos and people from other nations, and bring them into a community of disciples where we can grow together in faith. Pastor Fikri and Margot opened their home for a prayer meeting on Sunday morning, and people starting showing up. Our first prayer meeting was 5 people, the next was 14, and we continued to grow. Within a few month we couldn’t fit in the house anymore, and we moved to The offices of the Hispanic Family Foundation on Nolensville Pike, that graciously allowed us to use their facilities on Sunday mornings. For 6 month we met there every Sunday, worshipping the Lord together and growing in our faith. The question from day one was how can we minister and disciple people that spoke mainly Spanish and people that spoke mainly English? As we prayed and asked the Lord we were led to plant this church as a fully Bilingual church embracing the diversity of languages and cultures in our city. We do not tolerate the nations, we celebrate the nations among us! We enjoy worshiping in multiple languages, listening to the message in two language, and doing everything is a bilingual way.
