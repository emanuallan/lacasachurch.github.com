About Our Church

We Are a Bilingual Church

La Casa Church is a multiethnic, multigenerational community of disciples making disciples at home (Nashville) and in the nations. We believe that everyone needs a home and everyone needs a church, that is why we thrive to create an environment where you can experience God while feeling at home with us.
